About

In Bobby We Trust

Inspired by the viral trend of Bobby Kertanegara, the beloved cat of Indonesia’s President Prabowo Subianto, $BTc fosters a strong and engaged community. It unites supporters, cat lovers, and crypto enthusiasts under a shared vision of fun, inclusivity, and digital innovation.

Bobby the Cat ($BTc) is built on three core pillars: Community Token, Charity, and Collectible NFTs. It fosters a strong community-driven ecosystem, supports charitable causes, and offers exclusive NFT collections for collectors and supporters. Together, these pillars shape Bobby’s mission of impact, engagement, and digital innovation.
